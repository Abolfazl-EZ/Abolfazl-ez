    Simple Mango Mod (1.19, 1.18.2) – Enchanted Edibles for Effects

    1,217 views ❘ 2 hours ago ❘ Author: anvian ❘ Mod type: Fabric
    Share if you like
           1  
    1
    Share

    Simple Mango Mod (1.19, 1.18.2) introduces into the game a new type of tree, which grows a unique fruit that players can consume to grant special attributes. With these effects, players can utilise them to facilitate their journey in the world of Minecraft. At the same time, players can use these mangos to create enchanted attribution to integrate into their weapons. If players just want a quick solution to get new effects, players can also just straight-up consume these mangos to grant regeneration, strength, and many other unique buffs. Simple Mango will be a great solution for those who are too tired to hunt for another stack of Golden Apple.

    Features:

    • New Type of Fruit that players can consume for effects.
